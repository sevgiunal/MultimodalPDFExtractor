Look at the graph of monthly average temperatures recorded in Jamaica over a year. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
1. Which month had the highest average temperature? 
The correct answer is: C. August .  
The peak point in the graph is for the month of August 
 
2. Which month had the lowest average temperature? 
The correct answer is: B. February.  
 
The lowest point of the graph is in the month of February 
 
3. April and May have the same average temperature as June and July . 
The correct answer is: B. False. 
 
April and May have the same average temperature as November and December.
